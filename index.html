Instruction for Online Exam

Please Read The Instruction Carefully

  1. 1. Click Start Exam on the bottom of your screen to begin the test.
  2. 2. The clock has been set at server and the countdown timer at the top right side of the screen will display the remaining time to closure, from where you can monitor the time you have to complete the exam.
  3. 3. To answer a question, simply click the desired option button.
  4. 4. Candidates can change their response anytime during the examination slot by clicking another answer option. To remove an incorrect answer, click the desired option button again.
  5. 5. Click on Next to save the answer and move to the next question. The next question will automatically be displayed.
  6. 6. Click on Mark for Review to review your answer at a later stage.
  7. 7. To select a question directly, click on the question number on the right side of the screen.
  8. 8. The colour-coded diagram on the left side of the screen shows the status of each question.
